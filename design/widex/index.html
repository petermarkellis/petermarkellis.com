Widex

Widex creates and produces health tech technology specifically within the hearing aid industry.
A multi red dot winner for their innovative products.

  • Contribution

  • UI / UX & Interaction Design, Marketing

  • Project Duration

  • 2 Months

  • Platform

  • Fixed Tablet PoS Display

Whilst working at Widex I was seconded to help the marketing team develop and produce a PoS (point of sale) app that would be used at various outlets across Denmark and Europe that allows users to view the latest product range, trial the products and also request a hearing aid assistant to help with fittings. The agile team was super small but super lean consisting of an expensionally skilled developer Peter Ströiman, and a product manager Amalie Björnseon along with the wider marketing team. We managed to take on the marketing teams requirements and produce a simple fixed kiosk app running on an Apple iPad which was installed at their testing centers.
